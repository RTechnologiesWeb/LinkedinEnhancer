Recommendedations Regarding Education:

Leverage your education section to tell your professional story. Showcase your education strategically, prioritizing the most recent and relevant degrees. Include details like major, coursework, projects, and honors. Use the description to highlight skills and knowledge gained. List all relevant qualifications, even if some are not directly related to your current career goals. Always list your highest qualification as it showcases your academic level. Strategically include keywords in descriptions to enhance your profile's searchability. Highlight skills and knowledge acquired during your studies that are relevant to your desired career path. Include all higher education experiences, even if they aren't directly related, but omit high school details. For older degrees consider omitting the year of graduation: This can help prevent unconscious age bias.

Recommendations Regarding Licenses and Certifications:

The "Licenses & Certifications" section validates your expertise and boosts your profile's credibility. List all relevant certifications, including those beyond your primary profession, to showcase your diverse skillset. Consider adding a brief description highlighting the knowledge and skills acquired through each certification. Prioritize certifications most pertinent to your current career goals at the top. Remember: This section demonstrates your commitment to professional development and continuous learning, so keep it updated with new achievements
